So you know exactly what to expect.

How long does it take?

It depends on scope. The base landing-page offer is usually fast. If we add pages or upgrades, timeline adjusts clearly before work begins.

What do you need from me to start?

Your service type, location, ideal customer, and what actions you want most (calls, quote requests, bookings).

Can I start simple and upgrade later?

Yes. That’s the recommended path. Start with the core offer, then add pages, speed optimization, advanced hosting/security, or maintenance as needed.

Is hosting and SSL included?

Yes. The base offer includes hosting and SSL for 1 year. After that, each additional year is $100/year.
